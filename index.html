Difference between paddings, margins, and borders

Padding is the inner space between the content and the border of your box that is it controls how much breathing room exists within a box.

Margin is the outer space (or lack of space) surrounding the box that is it controls how much breathing room or whitespace exists outside of a box.

Border is the perimeter of the box. Borders can be invisible or they could be a thick colored line like the green one pictured above.
